Efficiency Score — Formula Reference
📐 isw Efficiency Score Formula (Support / Forethought tier)

Evaluates agent operational performance — rewards throughput, quality, and speed; penalizes escalations and slow resolution.

Efficiency Score = ( TC  ×  (1 − EC/TC)  ×  %FDR  ×  CSS )  ÷  ( IRT  ×  ADR )
VarNameDirectionTargetDefinition
TCTotal Cases▲ PositiveVolumeTotal support cases received
ECEscalated Cases▼ Negative< 5%Cases escalated to higher tiers
%FDRFirst Day Resolution %▲ Positive> 70%% of cases resolved day 1 (decimal)
CSSCustomer Satisfaction Score▲ Positive> 0.90Avg CSAT as decimal (0.82 = 82%)
IRTInitial Response Time▼ Negative< 1 hrAvg hours to first response
ADRAvg Days to Resolution▼ Negative< 2 daysAvg days to fully resolve
700+  Exceptional 500–699  Strong 300–499  Moderate < 300  Needs Improvement

Current inputs: TC=1,000 · EC=100 · %FDR=0.65 · CSS=0.82 · IRT=1.2 · ADR=2.4  →  Score = (1000 × 0.90 × 0.65 × 0.82) / (1.2 × 2.4) ≈ 167.8 raw  (normalized to 420 composite; early program, improving)
